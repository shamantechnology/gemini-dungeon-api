Strange occurrences plague a once-thriving mining town near a deep chasm. Whispers from the abyss echo through the tunnels, driving miners mad and warping reality. As the party explores the depths, they uncover a gateway to the Underdark and a cult seeking to unleash an ancient evil. The adventurers must navigate the treacherous underground, face aberrations, and prevent the impending doom that threatens to consume the surface world.
